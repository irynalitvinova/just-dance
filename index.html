About Us

For 10 years, Just Dance has been introducing the art of dance to people of all ages!

Just Dance Studio was launched in 2010. Over the years, we have become a close-knit artistic troupe, and many of the women and men who had attended our classes became participants in world dance shows. Just Dance Studio has the best teachers and a huge number of types of dances. We will help you immerse yourself in the music, and not only hear but also feel it!

Our History

The history of Just Dance Studio began in 2010. It was then that the idea came up to open a studio that would teach adults to dance. At first, classes were held in a small room, and the first group included only 6 people. In 10 years, we have moved to a large, professional ball-room and welcome students of different ages and levels!

Who founded Just Dance Studio?

Our owner is Anna Gonzales. She studied dancing in Spain, in the homeland of flamenco, bolero, saraband, seguidilla and a huge number of other dances. Anna is not only the director, but she also teaches tango and is a competition choreographer.
